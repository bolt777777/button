Bodyguard — your personal safety companion that connects you with professional security guards in seconds.

INSTANT SOS ALERT
Press the SOS button when you feel unsafe. Your GPS location is immediately sent to the security operations center. A guard is automatically dispatched to your exact location.

LIVE GUARD TRACKING
After sending an SOS, see the assigned guard approaching you on a real-time map. Track their movement, estimated arrival time, and distance — so you always know help is on the way.

PROFESSIONAL SECURITY NETWORK
Our platform connects you to a network of verified, professional security guards operating in your area. Each guard is GPS-tracked and ready to respond within minutes.

OPERATOR DISPATCH CENTER
Behind the scenes, trained operators monitor all active alerts on a live dashboard. They can manually assign guards, coordinate responses, and ensure your safety.

KEY FEATURES:
- One-tap SOS with 3-second countdown (prevents accidental triggers)
- Real-time map showing guard location and route
- Automatic nearest-guard assignment
- Live GPS tracking with ETA
- Device status monitoring (network, GPS, battery)
- Direct call to security center
- Secure JWT authentication
- Multi-language support (English, Russian)

PRIVACY & SECURITY
Your location is only shared when you activate an SOS alert. All data is encrypted in transit. We never sell your personal information.
